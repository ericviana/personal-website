About me

As far as I can remember I was always the curious kid who loves to disassemble stuff to find out what is inside and try to make new things. I've always been obsessed with the possibility of innovating, this obsession is what drives my life. So, as an inventor by nature and passion, I believe in the power of changing people's life by only using our minds.

I am deeply interested in the learning process, having as a hobby of mine learning languages. Native Brazilian Portuguese speaker, I can also speak more 2 languages fluently (you can see below on my skills section). Discovering new things and having new challenges is also something that really gets my attention.

Nowadays, my work ranges from creating interactive interfaces using design thinking principles, research and analyzing user needs for desktop and mobile applications to coding them as well using html, css, vuejs and many other tools. With these qualifications, I will try to manage every task that will be given to me.
